About FoodReconnect

Every year in Singapore, food waste remains one of the largest contributors to environmental impact, with over 813,000 tonnes wasted in 2022 alone. Much of this comes from large-scale catered events where organisers over-order to prevent food shortages.

FoodReconnect is a community-driven solution designed to bridge this gap. Our platform helps event organisers like corporate planners, wedding coordinators, and community managers quickly and easily share surplus food with nearby individuals who can make use of it.

At the end of an event, food providers simply upload a description, image, and location of available food. Our system uses geolocation technology to connect this surplus with users in the area who can opt to pick it up‚Äîreducing waste and benefiting the community.
